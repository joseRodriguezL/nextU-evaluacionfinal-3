Bad Bunny
Benito Antonio Martínez Ocasio, known by his stage name Bad Bunny, is a Puerto Rican Latin trap & reggaeton singer. While working in a supermarket as a bagger and studying at the University of Puerto Rico at Arecibo, Bad Bunny gained popularity on SoundCloud.
Born: March 10,1994(age 24)
Years Active: 2015-present
Associated acts: Daddy Yankee,Becky G,Cardi B, J Balvin,Prince Royce,Nicky Jam,Enrique Iglesias,Farruko,Ozuna,Karol G,Arcangel,Neñgo Flow

Ozuna

Juan Carlos Ozuna Rosado,known by his stage name Ozuna,is a Puerto Rican Latin trap & reggaeton singer.He started writing songs in his early teens and made his debut as a singer with the single ‘Imaginando’. He eventually signed a contract with Golden Family Records and also began to release his works on YouTube.

Born: March 13,1992
Years Active: 2012-present
Associated acts: Anuel AA,Arcangel,De LaGhetto,Kendo Kaponi,Farruko,Karol G,Wisin,Yandel,Cosculluela,Daddy Yankee,Romeo Santos,Cardi B,Natti Natasha,Akon
